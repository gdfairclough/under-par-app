Focus on the Game

Under Par provides a simple scorekeeping solution for golf and disc golf players that want to focus on the game, not the app. Get right down to playing with just a few taps.

No user accounts or personal information required. We believe your golf game is private, and we've designed Under Par to keep it that way. The app doesn't track your location, collect your data, or require any personal information whatsoever.

Navigate through the app with intuitive gestures and clear visual cues. View your complete scorecard at a glance, with easy-to-read hole-by-hole breakdowns that help you track your progress throughout the round.

Start a new round in seconds, add players as needed, and adjust course details on the fly. Whether you're playing a quick 9-hole round or a full 18-hole championship course, Under Par adapts to your needs without getting in the way of your golf experience.

Built for Everyone

Perfect for Groups

Manage multiple players seamlessly. Add friends mid-round or remove players who couldn't finish. Great for casual games with varying group sizes.

Any Course, Any Time

Works for traditional golf and disc golf. Whether it's 9 holes, 18 holes, or something custom, Under Par handles it all. From municipal courses to championship layouts, we've got you covered.

Your Data Stays Yours

No cloud sync, no data collection, no privacy concerns. Your rounds are stored locally on your device, exactly where they belong. What happens on the course stays on your device.
